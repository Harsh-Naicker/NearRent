About NearRent

NearRent is a peer-to-peer local commodity sharing marketplace. This platform allows the seller to upload various goods they wish to lend and the rentee to opt for the items they wish to rent. This web application connects people over a local network, so that a person who wishes to rent can have an open platform to do so.

Lend / Rent

Choose to either lend commodities to our community of users on a per-day cost basis or surf through the existing products available for rent and avail the benefits of our rich local rental business.
